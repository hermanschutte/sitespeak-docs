You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **ezyCourse** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to ezyCourse with SiteSpeakAI.

**ezyCourse** is an online course platform designed for educators and trainers to easily create and host their courses. It offers a straightforward approach to building and selling online learning experiences.

You can find more information at: [https://www.ezycourse.com/](https://www.ezycourse.com/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **ezyCourse** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.

![Copy code snippet](/images/installing-your-chatbot/ezycourse/0-Copy-Code.png)

### In ezyCourse

1. Log into your **ezyCourse** account and on the dashboard select **Website Builder** on the left sidebar

![Select Website Builder](/images/installing-your-chatbot/ezycourse/1-ezycourse-select-Website-Builder.png)

Note that you can add the AI support agent globally to your ezyCourse website or to specific pages

#### A. Adding your agent globally 

2. Select **Global JS Script** on the list under **Website Builder**

![Select Global JS Script](/images/installing-your-chatbot/ezycourse/2-select-Globa-JS-Script.png)

3. Insert your AI support agent snippet in the **Footer Script** (or the **Header Script**) textbox and click on Save

![Insert agent script globally](/images/installing-your-chatbot/ezycourse/3-insert-your-snippet.png)

#### B. Adding your agent to specific pages

4. Select **Pages** on the list under **Website Builder** and click on the **Edit Page Settings** option (3 vertical dots menu) for the page you want to add your agent to

![Select Edit for a Page](/images/installing-your-chatbot/ezycourse/4-select-edit-for-page.png)

5. Select the **Scripts** tab on the Page Settings, insert your AI support agent snippet in the **Footer Script** (or the **Header Script**) textbox and click on Save

![Insert snippet per Page](/images/installing-your-chatbot/ezycourse/5-insert-snippet-on-page-and-Save.png)

#### C. Display of the AI support agent (globally or page specific) 

6. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **ezyCourse** website page

![Agent button displayed](/images/installing-your-chatbot/ezycourse/6-agent-button.png)

7. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue

![Agent dialogue](/images/installing-your-chatbot/ezycourse/7-agent-dialogue.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **ezyCourse** website (globally or page specific) and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
