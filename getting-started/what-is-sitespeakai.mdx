## Key features

- **Custom training**: Train your AI on your specific content, FAQs, and documentation
- **No-code setup**: Get started in minutes without any technical expertise
- **Brand customization**: Match your chatbot's appearance to your brand
- **Analytics**: Track conversations, measure performance, and identify knowledge gaps
- **Multi-language**: Support customers in their preferred language

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your content">
    Add your website URLs, upload documents, or connect your knowledge base. SiteSpeakAI automatically crawls and indexes your content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Train your chatbot">
    Our AI learns from your content to understand your business, products, and common customer questions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy anywhere">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor and improve">

  </Step>
