You can easily use your chatbot to capture new leads and grow your sales.

## Enable lead capture

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Head over to the **Settings** section in your chatbot dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Leads tab">
    Click on the **Leads** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable lead capture">
    Toggle on the **Enable Lead Capture** option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your settings">
    Click the **Save** button to save your settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

  <img src="/images/getting-started/lead-capture-settings.png" alt="Lead capture settings" />

## Add custom fields

You can add your own custom fields that you would like your visitors to complete before they start chatting to your chatbot.

Common fields include:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Company name

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started/lead-capture-custom-fields.png" alt="Custom fields configuration" />
</Frame>

## Customise the form text

You can update the text displayed on the lead capture form by editing the Lead Capture text in the **Localization** settings.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started/lead-capture-localization.png" alt="Localization settings" />
</Frame>

## How it works

Once enabled, your chatbot will prompt visitors to leave their details before starting the chat.
